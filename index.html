Aastha Nigam

I am a 5th year Ph.D Student at University of Notre Dame in the Computer Science and Engineering Department. My research focusses on mining interesting patterns from users online behavior to improve their experience and make useful recommendations. I primarily work with data obtained from social media, online healthcare websites, social science and telecommunication companies. My work is context driven and lies at the intersection of data mining, machine learning and text analytics.
